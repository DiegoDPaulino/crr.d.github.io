About us

C&R Refrigeration is a leading refrigeration service company dedicated to providing top-notch solutions for businesses and individuals alike. Specializing in the installation, maintenance, and repair of commercial and residential refrigeration systems, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service that ensures your cooling needs are met with precision.

What can we do for you?

We specialized in a variety of jobs, including but not limited to the repairment of almost all appliances, stoves, fridges, microwaves, blenders etc. However, where we shine the most is in the installation, maintenance, and repair of commercial and residential refrigeration systems.
